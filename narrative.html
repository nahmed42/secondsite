Who is Nihad?

I’m Nihad Ahmed, a passionate English graduate student and a third grade teacher.

What do I aim for?

I aim to inspire others with the beauty of language! Nature and spending time outdoors also bring me peace and creativity.
What is joy to me?
Traveling brings me the greatest joy and always keeps me grounded. I have lived in 7 countries before I moved to the states at the age of 11 and lived in Virginia ever since. Join me on this exciting journey of learning!
